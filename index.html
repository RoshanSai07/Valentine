💌 The True Story of Valentine’s Day 💖

Valentine’s Day is more than just chocolates and roses—it has a fascinating history!

It dates back to ancient Rome, where Emperor Claudius II banned marriages for young soldiers, believing they fought better when single. A kind-hearted priest, St. Valentine, secretly performed marriages for young lovers. When the emperor found out, he sentenced Valentine to death.

While in prison, Valentine fell in love with the jailer’s daughter and sent her a letter signed “From Your Valentine” before his execution on February 14th, 269 AD. This phrase became the inspiration behind the love notes we exchange today.

Centuries later, Pope Gelasius I declared February 14th as St. Valentine’s Day, honoring love and devotion. Over time, it evolved into the romantic celebration we know today.

So whether you’re in love, single, or just here for the memes, Happy Valentine’s Day! ❤️
